FITLOG

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

FitLog ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal fitness tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the FitLog mobile app and web app.

Summary

FitLog does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data you enter — workouts, body measurements, nutrition logs, goals, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

Data We Do Not Collect

FitLog does not collect or transmit:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data to third-party servers or services

There are no user accounts, no sign-in, and no backend server storing your data.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is created by you and stored only on your device:

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weight)
  • Body measurements
  • Nutrition and meal logs
  • Goals and templates
  • App settings (weight unit, increment preferences)

This data is stored using your device's local storage. It is never transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Health Data (Apple Health & Google Health Connect)

FitLog can optionally read workout and activity data from Apple Health (iOS) or Google Health Connect (Android) to import your exercise history into the app.

  • This access requires your explicit permission.
  • Health data is only read — FitLog does not write to Apple Health or Health Connect.
  • Imported health data is stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not share health data with any third party.
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in your device's Health settings.

Third-Party Services

FitLog does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

FitLog is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
